## ?? HIGH-DEMAND SERVICES FOR SMALL GARAGES

### 1. **MOBILE/CALL-OUT SERVICES** ??????
**Revenue Potential:** HIGH | **Ease:** Medium

#### A. Battery Jump Start & Replacement
- **Price:** €50 call-out + €80-150 for battery
- **Description:** "Flat battery? We come to you. Jump start or same-day replacement."
- **Booking:** Emergency call-out button with instant WhatsApp

#### B. Roadside Emergency Assistance
- **Price:** €60-100 depending on location
- **Services:** 
  - Flat tire change
  - Fuel delivery (ran out of petrol/diesel)
  - Key lockout assistance
  - Overheating assistance
- **Marketing:** "Stranded on Achill? We'll get you moving within 30 minutes"

#### C. Mobile Pre-Purchase Inspections
- **Price:** €80-120
- **Description:** "Buying a used car? We'll inspect it at the seller's location before you commit"
- **Target:** Buyers from DoneDeal, Carzone

### 2. **TYRE SERVICES** ????
**Revenue Potential:** HIGH | **Ease:** Easy

#### A. Tyre Sales & Fitting
- **Price:** €60-200 per tyre + €10-15 fitting per tyre
- **Brands:** Budget to premium (Michelin, Continental, Hankook)
- **Booking System:** Select tyre size ? See available stock ? Book fitting slot

#### B. Puncture Repair
- **Price:** €15-25
- **Quick Service:** "In and out in 20 minutes"

#### C. Wheel Alignment
- **Price:** €50-80
- **Upsell:** Combine with tyre rotation (€30)

### 3. **BATTERY SERVICES** ????
**Revenue Potential:** Medium-High | **Ease:** Easy

#### A. Battery Health Check
- **Price:** FREE (upsell to replacement)
- **Marketing:** "Free battery test with any service"

#### B. Battery Replacement
- **Price:** €80-200 depending on vehicle
- **Warranty:** Include manufacturer warranty info

### 4. **AIR CONDITIONING SERVICES** ????
**Revenue Potential:** Medium | **Seasonal:** Summer Peak

#### A. AC Regas/Recharge
- **Price:** €60-100
- **Description:** "AC not cold? We'll have it icy again in under an hour"

#### B. AC System Diagnostics
- **Price:** €40 (waived if service booked)

### 5. **BRAKE SERVICES** ??????
**Revenue Potential:** HIGH | **Safety Critical:** YES

#### A. Brake Pad Replacement
- **Price:** €120-250 (front/rear)
- **Urgency:** Safety-focused messaging

#### B. Brake Fluid Change
- **Price:** €50-70
- **Frequency:** Every 2 years recommended

#### C. Brake Disc Replacement
- **Price:** €200-400
- **Upsell:** Combine with pads

### 6. **TIMING BELT REPLACEMENT** ????????
**Revenue Potential:** VERY HIGH | **Critical Service:** YES

- **Price:** €400-800 depending on vehicle
- **Marketing:** "Don't risk engine damage. We'll replace your timing belt at manufacturer intervals"
- **Value Add:** Include water pump replacement in quote

### 7. **HEADLIGHT RESTORATION** ??
**Revenue Potential:** Low-Medium | **Ease:** Very Easy

- **Price:** €40-60
- **Quick Win:** "Cloudy headlights? We'll make them crystal clear in 30 minutes"
- **Before/After Photos:** Perfect for social proof

### 8. **DETAILING PARTNER REFERRAL** ??
**Revenue Potential:** Referral Commission | **Ease:** Very Easy

- Partner with local car wash/detailer
- Offer combined "Service + Detail" packages
- Commission: 10-15% referral fee

### 9. **FLEET SERVICES** ????????
**Revenue Potential:** VERY HIGH | **Recurring:** YES

#### Target Businesses:
- Achill Island hotels (shuttle vans)
- Tour companies
- Delivery vans
- Trade contractors

#### Services:
- **Scheduled Maintenance Plans:** €200-500/month per vehicle
- **Priority Booking:** Dedicated fleet slots
- **Paperwork Management:** NCT reminders, insurance docs

### 10. **CLASSIC CAR SPECIALIST** ????
**Revenue Potential:** Medium-High | **Niche:** YES

- **Target:** Vintage car owners on Achill
- **Services:** 
  - Restoration project management
  - Sourcing rare parts
  - Winter storage inspections

### 11. **TOURIST VEHICLE ASSISTANCE** ????
**Revenue Potential:** Seasonal HIGH | **Summer Peak:** YES

#### A. Rental Car Damage Assessments
- **Price:** €50
- **Description:** "Hit a pothole in your rental? Get an independent assessment before returning it"

#### B. Pre-Road Trip Safety Checks
- **Price:** €40
- **Marketing:** "Driving the Wild Atlantic Way? Let us check your vehicle first"

### 12. **HOME CHARGING POINT INSTALLATION (EV)** ??????

## ?? SERVICE PRESENTATION STRATEGY

### Interactive Service Cards
Each service should have:
- ? **Icon/Image:** Visual representation
- ? **Service Name:** Clear and simple
- ? **Price Range:** €XX - €XX (transparency)
- ? **Duration:** "1-2 hours" or "Same day"
- ? **Book Now Button:** Direct to GHL calendar with service pre-selected
- ? **Learn More:** Expandable details
- ? **Loyalty Points:** "Earn 100 points"

### Booking Flow
```
Service Card ? Click "Book Now" ? GHL Calendar (filtered to service type) 
? Select date/time ? Enter details ? Confirmation ? GHL automation ? SMS/Email reminder
```
