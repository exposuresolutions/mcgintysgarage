More Than Just a Mechanic

Ethan Lavelle isn't just fixing cars; he's continuing a legacy.

Since 1970, the Lavelle family has been keeping Achill moving. Ethan grew up in this garage, learning the trade from his grandfather before formally qualifying as a master mechanic at 27.

Today, he combines those time-honoured techniques with cutting-edge diagnostic technology. He believes in the old-school value of fixing a component rather than just replacing it, saving you money and reducing waste.
